--- layout: default title: Formula 1 Driver Knowledge in Wikidata --- # 🏁 Formula 1 Driver Knowledge in Wikidata This project explores Wikidata entries of several Formula 1 drivers using **SPARQL queries**, **RDF triples**, and **Large Language Models** (LLMs). We analyzed data about driver nationalities, teams, achievements, and relationships — and identified key **gaps in information**. To fill these gaps, we used LLMs (ChatGPT & Gemini) to retrieve facts and suggest RDF triples. These were then verified and manually corrected, ensuring **semantic accuracy and alignment with the Wikidata ontology**. --- ## 👤 Selected Drivers - **Lewis Hamilton** - **Charles Leclerc** - **Kimi Antonelli** - **Max Verstappen** --- ## 📑 Sections - [SPARQL & Prompts](sparql-prompts.md) - [Methodology](methodology.md) - [Driver Cases](hamilton.md) - [Final Report](report.md) - [Challenges](challenges.md)
